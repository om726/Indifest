Top Tourist Destinations in India

Explore the most popular tourist places across India, along with nearby attractions and budget-friendly travel options.

Kashmir

Nearby Attractions: Dal Lake, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonamarg

Nearest City: Srinagar (10-15 minutes from Srinagar Airport)

Travel Tip: Best visited in the summer (May-July) or winter (December-February) for skiing and snow activities.

Budget Travel Options

Ladakh

Nearby Attractions: Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, Tso Moriri Lake, Leh Palace

Nearest City: Leh (Leh Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport)

Travel Tip: Best visited in summer (May-August) for road trips to explore the landscapes.

Budget Travel Options

Delhi

Nearby Attractions: Qutub Minar, Red Fort, Humayun's Tomb, India Gate

Nearest City: Central Delhi (Accessible from Indira Gandhi International Airport)

Travel Tip: Visit Old Delhi for a traditional experience and try the street food!

Budget Travel Options

Sikkim

Nearby Attractions: Tsomgo Lake, Nathula Pass, Pelling, Yumthang Valley

Nearest City: Gangtok (120 km from Bagdogra Airport)

Travel Tip: The region is famous for its monasteries and scenic beauty.

Budget Travel Options

Meghalaya

Nearby Attractions: Cherrapunji, Dawki, Mawlynnong, Shillong

Nearest City: Shillong (Accessible by Guwahati Airport)

Travel Tip: Visit the living root bridges and waterfalls!

Budget Travel Options

Kerala Backwaters

Nearby Attractions: Kochi, Munnar, Alappuzha

Nearest City: Kochi (1 hour from Kochi International Airport)

Travel Tip: Take a houseboat cruise for a truly unique experience!

Budget Travel Options

Andaman Islands

Nearby Attractions: Radhanagar Beach, Cellular Jail, Havelock Island

Nearest City: Port Blair (Port Blair International Airport)

Travel Tip: A paradise for beach lovers and scuba diving enthusiasts.

Budget Travel Options

Goa Beaches

Nearby Attractions: Baga Beach, Anjuna Beach, Fort Aguada

Nearest City: Panaji (30 minutes by car)

Travel Tip: Visit during the off-season (May - September) for a quieter experience.

Budget Travel Options

Agra

Nearby Attractions: Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, Fatehpur Sikri

Nearest City: Delhi (4 hours by road)

Travel Tip: Plan your visit early in the morning to avoid the crowds.

Budget Travel Options

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Nearby Attractions: Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Sarnath, Dashashwamedh Ghat

Nearest City: Prayagraj (3 hours by road)

Budget Travel Options

Jaisalmer
