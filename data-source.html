Where We Get Our Data

Our festival information and details about tourist destinations are sourced from various reputable and trusted sources. Below is a list of these sources:

Festival Data Sources

  • Government Websites: Official tourism and cultural department sites provide reliable data on national holidays and festivals.
    • India Government Portal
    • Incredible India
  • Travel Blogs and Websites: Many travel bloggers provide first-hand experiences and details on festivals across India.
    • TripAdvisor
    • Lonely Planet
  • Festival Organizers: Direct information from festival organizers or event coordinators.
    • Dussehra Mela
    • Pushkar Camel Fair
  • Wikipedia: A vast resource of information about the history, celebrations, and significance of festivals.
    • Dussehra Festival - Wikipedia
    • Holi Festival - Wikipedia

Tourist Places Data Sources

  • Tourism Boards: Local tourism boards and government portals provide the best and most reliable information on tourist destinations.
    • Incredible India - Official Tourism Portal
    • Maharashtra Tourism
  • Travel Websites and Agencies: These websites provide detailed descriptions, images, and user reviews of tourist spots.
    • TripAdvisor
    • Booking.com - Travel Guides
  • Google Travel and Reviews: Google reviews and maps help provide real-time data about tourist destinations and user experiences.
    • Google Maps
    • Google Travel
  • Wikipedia: Wikipedia contains a wealth of knowledge on famous landmarks, historical sites, and tourism facts.
    • Taj Mahal - Wikipedia
    • Red Fort - Wikipedia
